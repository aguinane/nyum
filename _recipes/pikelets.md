* 3/4 cups of milk
* 1 egg
* 1 cup SR flour, sifted
* 1 tbsp caster sugar
* 1 tsp vanilla essence


> Beat milk, egg, sugar and vanilla 

---

> Add in flour

---

> Spoon mixture into frying pan

---

> Cook until golden

---

> Serve with fresh fruit, honey and cream
