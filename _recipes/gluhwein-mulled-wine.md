* `3 bottles` cheap red wine
* `2 cups` water

> Add cheap red wine and water to a large pot.

---

* `10` cloves
* `2` cinnamon sticks
* `1/4 cup` sugar
* `1` grated nutmeg
* `2` cut up oranges

> Mix in cloves, cinnamon sticks, sugar, grated nutmeg, cut up oranges.

---

> Warm to a low heat (avoid boiling!)

---

> Serve with ladle into mugs
