Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups of self-raising flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 2 tbsp of cocoa
  • 1 tsp of vanilla essence

Mix everything together with beaters Bake for 20-30mins Add chocolate icing once cool

Enjoy!
