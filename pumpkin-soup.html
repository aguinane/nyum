Pumpkin Soup

Tasty entree and great for babies.

  • 1.5kg pumpkin, peeled and cut into large cubes
  • 2 tomatoes halved
  • 1 large onion, peeled and quartered
  • 5 cups water
  • 5 chicken stock cubes
  • pinch salt
  • cayenne pepper (optional)

Place tomatoes and onion into food processor bowl and process until finely chopped. Combine pumpkin, tomato and onion mixture with water, chicken stock cubes in a pan. Simmer gently until pumpkin is tender approx >0 mins. Puree pumpkin mixture until smooth in food processor. Serve soup sprinkled with parsley and greek yoghurt.

Enjoy!
