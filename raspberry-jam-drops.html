Raspberry Jam drops

  • 175g margarine, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 icing sugar, sifted
  • 1 cup plain flour, sifted
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • raspberry jam

Preheat oven to 180 degrees and line a baking tray with paper Beat margarine, vanilla and icing sugar until fluffy Add flour and cornflour and mix until dough forms With lightly floured hands roll 1 tbsp size dough and place on baking tray Make an indent in the dough with your finger Spoon raspberry jam into hole Bake for 10 mins

Enjoy!
