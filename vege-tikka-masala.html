Vege Tikka Masala

  • sweet potato or potato
  • onion
  • can of lentils
  • can of diced tomatoes
  • can of coconut cream
  • 3-4tbsp of tikka maslala paste
  • chickpeas (optional)
  • carrot (optional)

Slice potato and boil in microwave for 10 mins Saute onion, add tomatoes and paste and stir Add potato and coconut cream Simmer for 10 mins or until less watery Serve with naan and rice

Enjoy!
