Caponata Pasta

  • Meat
  • 1 eggplant, cut into 2cm pieces
  • olive oil
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • celery salt
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 80g parmesan cheese
  • 2 x 400g diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sultanas
  • 1 tbs red wine vinegar
  • pasta

Preheat oven to 200 degrees Bake eggplant for 20 mins Cook pasta as per package instructions Cook onion and celery for 10 mins Add garlic, oregano and half parmesan, tomatoes, sultanas and 1/2 cup of water Bring to the boil and then simmer uncovered for 8 mins Sitr in eggplant and vinegar Serve with fresh spinach and rocket and parmesan cheese

Enjoy!
