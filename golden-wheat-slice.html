Golden Wheat Slice

  • 1 cup SR flour
  • 4 crushed weet-bix
  • 1/2 cup sultanas
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 6 tbsp margarine
  • 1 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp margarine

Preheat oven to 180 degrees Grease slice tin with spray oil Add cruished weet-bix, flour, sultanas and brown sugar. Melt margarine in microwave. Add golden syrup and milk to margarine and stir. Add wet ingredients to flour mixture. Pour into slice tin and press into shape. Bake for 20-25 mins. To mkae icing combine icing sugar, lemon juice, milk and margaine. Pour over hot slice and leave to cool.

Enjoy!
