Naan Bread

  • 2 cup self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1/3 cup coconut
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • handfull of sultanas
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic

Mix dry ingredients together Add yogurt and mix well Knead into dough and roll Cook in pan for a couple of mins each side Add melted garlic butter on top

Not too hard to make to go with Indian.

Enjoy!
