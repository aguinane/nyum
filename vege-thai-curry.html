Vege Thai Curry

  • some onion
  • some capsicum

Saute onion and capsicum

  • 3-4tbsp red thai curry paste

Add red thai curry paste and stir

  • some green beans

Boil green beans in microwave

  • 1 can coconut cream

Add coconut cream

Simmer for 10 mins or until less watery

Serve with rice

Enjoy!
