Crustless Quiche

Preheat oven to 160 degrees

  • 3 eggs
  • `1/2 cup margarine, melted
  • `1/2 cup SR flour
  • `1 1/2 cup milk
  • cheese
  • optional bacon, spinach, sundried tomatoes, olives, peas, corn etc

Beat eggs Add flour, margarine and flour Add optional ingredrients Spoon into silicone muffins trays Bake for 10-20 mins depending on size of muffin trays

Enjoy!
