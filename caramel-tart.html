Caramel Tart

This recipe comes from Lauren who enjoys a good caramel tart

  • 1 packet of milk arrowroot biscuits
  • margarine
  • 1 can of Caramel condensed milk

Preheat oven to 180 degrees and grease tart pan Blend biscuits into a fine crumb Melt margarine and add to biscuit until it clumps togther Place in oven until hard Place in fridge for an hour Whisk caramel condensed milk Serve with whipped cream

Enjoy!
