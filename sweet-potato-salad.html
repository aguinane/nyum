Sweet Potato Salad

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 bag of spinach and rockets leaves
  • pine nutes or crushed cashews
  • feta cheese, crumbled
  • balsamic vinegar
  • honey
  • avocado

Preheat oven to 180 degrees Cut potato into 2 cubes Season with salt and pepper Roast for 30 mins or until soft Wash spinach and rocket leaves, and nuts to mix Combine balsamic vingear and honey and drizzle over salad Serve with feta and avocado

Enjoy!
