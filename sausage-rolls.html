Sausage Rolls

  • Meat
  • 500g mince (lamb, chicken, or turkey)
  • 2 slices of toast
  • 1 egg
  • Pastry sheets
  • seasonings and sauce

Preheat oven to 180 degrees Blend bread Using hands mix mince, egg and breads together Add seasoning Halve pastry sheets Place mince on pastry sheet, roll into log and cut. Brush pastry with milk Cook in oven for 25-30 mins, turning half way through

Enjoy!
