---
title: "Sushi"
category: mains
---


* `0.5 kg` sushi rice

> Cook sushi rice in rice cooker.

---

* `some` chicken tenders

> Cook chicken tenders in oven.

---

* `2 tbsp` rice vinegar

> Once rice cooked, mix in rice vinegar.

---

* `some` nori sheets

> Put nori sheets on bamboo mat.

---

> Spread cooked rice over nori sheets.

---

* `1` avocado

> Add chicken and avocado on top of rice.

---

> Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat and slice into pieces.

---

* `some` BBQ sauce
* `some` mayonnaise

> Top with BBQ sauce and mayonnaise.
