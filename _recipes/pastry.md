---
title: "Pastry"
category: components
---


* `2 1/2 cups` plain flour
* `1/2 teaspoon` salt
* `200 grams` butter

> Mix plain flour, salt and butter cut up into cubes in a bowl until crumbly.

---

* `1/4 cup` cold water

> Add about cold water and mix to form a dough.

---

> Roll out on a lightly floured surface.

---

> Bake for 15-20 minutes.
