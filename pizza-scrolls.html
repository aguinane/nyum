Pizza Scrolls

  • 2 1/2 cups self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp herbs
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • pizza sauce
  • cheese

Add flour to a bowl with butter and herbs Stir in milk with a knife to make dough But heaps of flour on surface and knead dough Roll into flat sheet Add sauce and cheese on top Roll into a roll and cut into pieces Bake in oven

These were pretty good. Make sure you make the roll doughed thin or it tastes too flourly.

Enjoy!
