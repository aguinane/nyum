title: "Overnight Porridge"
category: breakfasts

---


* `1/2 cup` oats
* `1 tbsp` chia seeds
* `1/2 cup` almond milk
* `1 tbsp` greek yoghurt

> Place oats, chia seeds, almond milk and greek yoghurt in a glass jar

---

> Refrigerate overnight

---

> Serve with your choice of toppings
