* `3 bottles` cheap red wine
* `10` cloves
* `2` cinnamon sticks
* `1/4 cup` sugar
* `1` grated nutmeg
* `2 cups` water
* `2` cut up oranges

> Combine cheap red wine , cloves, cinnamon sticks,  sugar grated nutmeg water cut up oranges  in large pot

---

> warm to low to medium heat (avoid boiling!) serve with ladle into mugs
