Lemon Gnocchi Traybake

  • Vegan
  • 1 bunch of brocclini
  • 10-15 brussel sprouts
  • 1 packet fresh gnocchi
  • olive oil
  • basil or parsley leaves
  • salt and pepper

Preheat oven to 220 degrees Grease oven tray Trim ends of brocclini and brussl sprouts, place in tray Place gnocchi, brussel sprouts and brocclini in tray Slice half of the lemon and place in tray Add the remaining half of lemon juice,to oil, salt and pepper and herbs to jug Pour mixture over gnocchi and toss Bake in oven for 30 mins Serve with fresh avocado and cheese
