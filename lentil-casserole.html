Moroccan-Style Lentil Casserole

  • 1 carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tin brown lentils, drained
  • 1 tin chickpeas, drained
  • 1 tin diced tomatoes
  • 1 red chilli
  • cumin
  • cinanmon
  • onion powder

Boil carrot and potato in microwave unitl soft Saute onion with spices in saucepan Add lentils, chickpeas, vegetables and diced tomatoes. Simmer until most of the liquid has evaporated. Serve with rice or lebanese bread, greek yoghurt and fresh coriander.

Enjoy!
