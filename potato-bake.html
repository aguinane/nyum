Potato Bake

  • Meat
  • 500g potatoes
  • 4 rashers of bacon
  • red onion, diced
  • 70g margarine
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 3 cups of milk

Preheat oven to 180 degrees, grease casserole dish Boil potatoes for 10 mins, then allow to cool Meanwhile fry bacon Slice cooled potaoes, layer in dish with bacon and onion To make sauce, melt margarine Add flour and slowly whisk in milk until a thick sauce develops Pour sauce over potato and sprinkle with cheese Bake in oven until golden
