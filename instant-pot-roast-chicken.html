Instant Pot Roast Chicken

  • Meat
  • whole chicken
  • choice of seasoning

Rub chicken with seasoning

Turn instant pot onto SAUTE function Sear both sides of chicken for 4-5mins

  • 1 cup vegetable broth

Place chicken on trivet Add broth Turn instant pot to MANUAL, HIGH function

Cooking time depends on weight of chicken, see notes Cook chicken under pressure for 6 mins per 500g, e.g. 1.3kg chicken for 25 mins or 1.8kg chicken for 30mins Allow natural release pressure for 15 mins

Enjoy!
