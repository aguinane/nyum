* `1 cups` plain flour
* `3 tbsp` sugar
* `1/4 tsp` salt

> Add plain flour, sugar and a pinch of salt in a large mixing bowl.

---

* `3` eggs

> Add eggs and whisk.

---

* `2 cups` milk
* `1/3 cup` water
* `2 tbsp` oil

> Gradually add milk water oil and whisk until smooth.

---

> Cover and leave for up to an hour to rest.

---

* `some` butter

> Put butter in a pan and cook for 1 minute then flip.
