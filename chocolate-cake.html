Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa

Mix dry ingregients in mixing bowl

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Mix in wet ingredients with beaters

Bake for 20-30mins

  • 1 tbsp cocoa
  • 1 cup icing mixture
  • 1 tbsp milk

Add chocolate icing once cool

Enjoy!
