Sweet Potato Mushroom & Two Bean Cottage Pie

  • 700g sweet potatoes, peeled, chopped
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 1 onion
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 400g diced tomatoes
  • 400g black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 400g red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp mexican mix

Steam sweet potato Mash sweet potatoes Heat oil in frying pan, cook onions for 5mins Add mushrooms and cook for another 5 mins Stir in beans, mexican mix and water Bring to the boil and then reduce heat and simmer for 8 mins Transfer mushroom and bean mix to baking dish Top with mashed potato Grill for 15-20 mins until sweet potato is starting to brown Serve with steamed green vegetables

Enjoy!
