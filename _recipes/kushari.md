* 2 cups pasta
* 1 1/2 cups rice
* 1 can chickpeas
* 1 can lentils
* 1 onion
* 4 clove garlic
* chilli
* cinnamon
* 1 can diced tomato
* 2 tbsp white vinegar
* salt & pepper
* 1/3 cup flour
* 1 onion


> Cook rice and pasta
> Brown onion, garlic
> Add diced tomato, vinegar and spices to sauce
> Mix rice, pasta, lentils, chickpeas
> Make fried onion by covering in flour and frying in oil
> (optionally) top with chicken or lamb
