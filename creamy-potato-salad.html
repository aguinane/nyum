Creamy Potato Salad

  • 1.5kg potatoes
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup paprika
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • shallots

Boil potatoes for 12-15mins Drain and leave to cool Combine mayonnaise, red onion, Dijon mustrad, lemon juice and paprika Stir through potatoes Sprinkle shallots over the top of mixture

Enjoy!
