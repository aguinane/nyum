Vegetable Lentil Pie

  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups frozen vegetable mix
  • curry powder
  • 1 can brown lentils
  • 1 tsp vegetable stock
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 pastry sheets
  • egg, beaten

Saute onions and garlic in saucepan Add curry powder and cook until fragant Add lentils and veg
Add stock and water and simmer Grease casserole dish Transfer pie contents to dish Cut pastry into strips Arrange in a lattice formation over pie Paint egg on pastry Cook until pastry is golden

Enjoy!
