* `4 cups` sugar
* `1 cup` milk

> Combine sugar with milk in a saucepan over low heat. Stir until sugar is dissolved.

---

> Bring sugar mixture to the boil, stirring for 5 minutes.

---

* `3 cups` coconut

> Mix in coconut and stir for another 5 minutes.

---

> Poor half of the mixture in a lined slice pan and put in frige.

---

> Add food colouring to the remaining mixutre.

---

> Pour the coloured mixture over the white layer in the slice pan.
