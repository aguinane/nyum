Bix Balls

  • 11 weetbix
  • 1 cup of coconut
  • 1/3 cup milo
  • 1 tin of condensed milk
  • extra cocnut for coating

place weetbix in blender and blend into a fine crumb add coconut, milo and condensed milk and mix scoop out mixture and roll into ball (if mixture if falling apart coat balls with extra coconut leave in fridge for 1 hour to set

Enjoy!
