Instant Pot Basic Risotto

  • Meat
  • choice of vegetables to roast
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas

Preheat oven to 200 degrees Cut vegetables into 2cm cubes and season Bake until golden

  • olive oil
  • margarine

Place oil and margarine in multicooker, press saute, allow to heat up for 1 min

  • 1 clove garlic

Add onion and garlic and saute for 2 mins

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups arborio rice

Add rice, stirring constantly

  • 1/2 cup of whte wine

Add wine and cook for 4 mins

  • 4 cups vegetable stock

Add stock Select manual, high setting and cook for 6 mins

Leave to warm for 5 mins Release pressure Add in roast vegetables

  • 1 tbsp lemon zest

Serve with lemon zest, salt and pepper

Enjoy!
