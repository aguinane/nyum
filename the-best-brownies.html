The Best Brownies

  • 125g butter (not margarine)
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 125g dark choclate - chooped
  • 3 eggs
  • 2/3 cup plain flour or almond meal
  • 1/3 cup coca powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 150g walnuts (optional)

Preheat oven to 180 degrees Greaze 18 x 28cm cake tin Place butter, sugar and chocolate in pan and melt on stove, sitrring regularly Remove from heat and beat in eggs (one at a time) with spoon Sift flour, coca butter anbd baking powder Stir until combined Stir in walnuts Pour into pan and bake for 35-40mins Sprinkle with icing sugar

Warning - these are extremely unhealthy but super tasty

Enjoy!
