* `3/4 cups` milk
* `1` egg
* `1 tbsp` caster sugar
* `1 tsp` vanilla essence

> Beat milk, egg, caster sugar and vanilla essence

---

* `1 cup` SR flour

> Add in SR flour

---

> Spoon mixture into frying pan

---

> Cook until golden

---

> Serve with fresh fruit, honey and cream
