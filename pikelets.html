Pikelets

  • 10 pikelets
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Beat egg, caster sugar and vanilla essence

  • 3/4 cups milk

Mix in milk

  • 1 cup SR flour

Add in SR flour

Spoon mixture into frying pan

Cook until golden

Serve with fresh fruit, honey and cream

Enjoy!
